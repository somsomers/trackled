## [1.0.0] - 2026-07-02

### Added

- Menu-bar time tracking per application and window title, aggregated by day.
- Statistics window with a date picker and ranked, collapsible app cards with
  progress bars.
- Weekly trend bar chart per application.
- App info popover (bundle ID, version, path, *Show in Finder*).
- Light / dark / system theme switch.
- Settings: configurable sampling interval and idle timeout.
- Launch-at-login toggle.
